Parallel Circut With Two Switches

For this project, we create a parallel circut that allows the red LED light to be powered on by either of the two buttons.

Parts Required For Assembly

  • Power Cables
  • 1 220ohm resistor
  • 1 Red LED Light
  • 2 Switches
  • 3 Small Jumper Wires
  • 1 Medium/Large Jumper Wire
