About Me

Well, I'm Destiny!

On the internet, I go by the username DawnlightDev (or sometimes just Dawnlight). I'm a 21 year old Computer Science and Mathematics undergraduate with a major passion for all aspects of Computer Science, but mostly in software engineering and design, as well as having a passion for various topics in Mathematics such as Algebra and Calculus. I work part-time as a MySQL database developer working on a Chinese pinyin dictionary database and also tutor the underclassmen in introductory computer science courses at my university. I'm a hobbyist game developer playing with C# and Godot and I have a vision to create and release my dream game at some point in my Computer Science career. My goal in Computer Science is to become a professor, so once I am done with my Bachelor's, I plan on going to graduate school to get my PhD.

I primarily work with Java, C# and Python on a near daily basis, but I have dabbled in other languages like JavaScript, Ruby, Lisp and PHP. I have certifications in Responsive Web Design and Foundational C# and am always more than willing to share my knowledge of Computer Science with anyone who will listen.
